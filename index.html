Our project idea originated during our exploration of Arduino circuit boards, and as we delved deeper into the concept, we found ourselves increasingly captivated. Fortunately,a well-timed opportunity to bring our idea to fruition presented itself during the gathering ceremony of all schools. We collectively pooled our pocket money, providing us with a budget to work with.The process of procuring materials and components proved to be a lengthy and challenging endeavor. Whether it was the limitation of our budget or the difficulty of reaching stores amidst our country's political situation, the search was arduous. Nevertheless, we persevered and managed to acquire all the necessary items. We faced hardships, embraced our occasional failures with smiles and laughter, and relied on our inventive minds. Mutual assistance and encouragement from peers, coupled with our unwavering determination to impress everyone at the gathering,propelled us forward. No obstacle could hinder our path to success in creation.
